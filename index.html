366MOVIES

A collection of short clips from 366 movies that I watched over the course of 1 year.

The process of collecting these clips made me appreciate more small nuances of cinematogaphy like camera movement, use of color, light, framing that tells a story, composition, meaning, storytelling through visuals.

There are no ratings, recommendations or reviews here. Some movies have a ☆ or ★ next to their title, which just indicates movies that I enjoyed the most.

Enjoy the collection.
